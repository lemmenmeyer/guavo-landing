Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This policy explains what personal information Guavo Inc. (“Guavo,” “we,” “us”) collects when you visit guavo.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. We wrote this in plain English on purpose.

Who we are

Guavo Inc. is a small-business finance company based in Miami, Florida. If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise any of your rights below, email us at contact@guavo.com.

What information we collect

The site has two data-collection surfaces:

1. Analytics (Google Analytics 4) — only after you consent via the cookie banner. Once you accept, GA4 collects:

  • The pages you visit on guavo.com and how long you spend on each
  • The site or link that referred you (e.g., google.com, linkedin.com, an email link)
  • Approximate geography derived from your IP address (city or region level, IP itself is truncated before storage)
  • Device type, browser, screen size, and operating system
  • Events you trigger, such as submitting the application form or downloading the PDF

Before you consent, GA4 sends anonymous, cookieless signals that let us count aggregate visits without identifying you or storing anything on your device. If you reject, no cookies are set and no identifiers are stored.

2. Application form — if you fill out and submit the application form on the site, we collect the fields you provide: business name, owner name, contact email and phone, financing amount requested, and any notes. This data is sent to us via EmailJS (a third-party form-submission service) and stored in our email inbox. We use it to review your financing application. We do not sell or share this data with marketers.

What we deliberately do not collect

  • No Meta / Facebook pixel, no LinkedIn Insight Tag, no TikTok pixel, no advertising cookies of any kind.
  • No session recordings or heatmaps.
  • No fingerprinting or “cookieless tracking” techniques designed to circumvent consent.
  • No selling of your data. No sharing with data brokers.

How long we keep the data

Analytics event data is retained in Google Analytics for 14 months, then deleted automatically. Aggregate, non-identifiable reports may be kept longer. Application form submissions are retained as long as needed to evaluate your financing request and to comply with our legal record-keeping obligations, then deleted on request.

Legal basis (GDPR)

If you are in the EU or UK, our legal basis for processing is your consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)) for analytics, and the necessity to take steps at your request prior to entering a contract (Article 6(1)(b)) for application-form submissions. You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting past processing.

Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Know what information we hold about you
  • Correct or update it
  • Have it deleted
  • Receive a copy of it (portability)
  • Withdraw your consent for analytics
  • Opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information (see below)

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@guavo.com. We’ll respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

We do not sell your personal information for money. We also do not “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined under the California Privacy Rights Act. In other words: we have nothing to opt out of on the “Do Not Sell or Share” front, because we don’t do it in the first place. If you still want to withdraw analytics consent, click . We also honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signal.

Residents of other US states

If you live in Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, Tennessee, Delaware, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Iowa, Indiana, or any other state with a comprehensive consumer privacy law, the rights listed above apply to you as well. Email contact@guavo.com to exercise them.

Third parties we use

  • Google (Google Analytics 4) — analytics processor. Data is anonymized and Google Signals is disabled. Google’s privacy policy.
  • EmailJS — delivers your application form submission to our inbox. EmailJS privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the website. Vercel privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads the fonts you see. Note: this triggers a request to Google’s font servers on page load. If this concerns you, most browsers offer content-blocking extensions that can prevent it.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle your data, we’ll update this page and, when the change is significant, prompt you to review it. The “Last updated” date at the top always reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: contact@guavo.com. If you’re in the EU and prefer to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, you can do so with the data protection authority of your country of residence.
